what's my passcode again?


how to play

guess the passcode in 6 tries.

each guess must be a valid 5-number combination (numbers can repeat)

the color of the tiles will change according to the accuracy of your number

example

1
5
3
2
6

1 and 3 are in the passcode and in the right spots
5 and 2 are in the passcode but not in the right spots
6 is not in the passcode
